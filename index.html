Westbridge Forensic Advisory was founded to address a specific gap in the market: the need for a boutique firm that combines deep financial-modeling discipline with genuine investigative expertise — and that operates with the independence and objectivity that litigation and regulatory contexts demand.

We work at the intersection of finance, law, and compliance. Our clients engage us when they need answers that cannot be approximate — when the stakes are too high for uncertainty, when the opposing party will challenge every figure, and when the credibility of the analysis matters as much as its conclusions.

We are a deliberately small firm. We do not sub-contract our work or dilute it through layers of junior staff. Every engagement is handled by senior practitioners who are accountable for the methodology, the findings, and the testimony it may produce.

Our Methodology

A Disciplined Process.
Defensible Results.

Every engagement follows a structured, documented methodology — because the process is as important as the conclusion.

01
Intake & Scoping
A confidential initial assessment to define the matter, understand what is at stake, establish the evidentiary standard required, and agree on a precise scope of work.
02
Investigation & Analysis
Forensic review of financial records, transactional data, and documentary evidence. We follow the money — tracing flows, modeling scenarios, and testing hypotheses against the data.
03
Findings & Reporting
Clear, structured reporting calibrated to the audience — whether legal counsel, a board, a regulator, or a court. Every conclusion is traceable to its evidentiary basis.
04
Resolution Support
Expert testimony, regulatory presentation support, or assistance in negotiation and settlement — we remain available through the full resolution of the matter.
